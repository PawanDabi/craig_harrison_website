Selected Works — Residential

The Glass
Pavilion

A study in transparency and form, the Glass Pavilion stands at the edge of the South Downs, where the horizon dissolves into sky. The structure is not a house — it is a frame for landscape; a threshold between the inhabited and the infinite.

The brief called for a home that would honour its setting without competing with it. Our response was a linear pavilion of glass and exposed concrete, oriented precisely to capture both the morning light and the distant view of the sea. Every window is a painting. Every threshold is a ceremony.
